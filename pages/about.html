Our Beginnings

Born in the Royal Courts of Jaipur

Maharani was born from the needle and thread of Jaipur's royal embroiderers — artisans whose families had served the Maharajas for generations. In a world that moved toward mass production, we chose to move deeper into craft.

Each piece we create passes through at least twelve pairs of hands before it reaches you. From the weavers who set the base fabric to the zardozi masters who lay the gold, every step is a meditation on excellence.

Our Philosophy

Luxury That Transcends Time

We believe that true luxury is not in the price tag — it is in the intention. Every Maharani piece is designed to be worn once and treasured forever; to be passed from mother to daughter, carrying with it the stories of the celebrations it witnessed.

We source only the finest silk, velvet and organza from heritage looms across India, and our 22k gold threads are procured directly from Rajasthan's oldest gold traders.
